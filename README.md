# Safety Training and Examination安全教育培训及考试管理系统

#### 系统介绍
教育培训及考试管理系统是一套面向高校、科研院所、教育培训机构及企事业单位的全流程、数字化安全教育管理平台。系统严格依据国家安全生产法律法规及行业标准，聚焦实验室安全、特种设备操作、生产作业等高风险场景，全面支撑“先培训、后考核、再准入”的安全管理机制。
平台深度融合在线学习、培训管理、模拟练习、在线考试、成绩分析与证书管理六大核心功能，构建“教、学、练、考、管”一体化的闭环管理体系。覆盖学员管理、课程分配、在线培训、学习跟踪、理论与实操考试、电子证书颁发、档案归档及复审提醒等全生命周期流程，实现安全教育全过程的数字化、标准化、可追溯、可量化管理。
通过落实准入前强制培训与考核机制，系统有效提升人员安全素养、规范操作行为、增强应急处置能力，为科研、教学和生产活动筑牢安全防线，助力单位切实履行安全生产主体责任。

#### English version introduction

#### System Introduction

The education, training, and examination management system is a full process, digital security education management platform aimed at universities, research institutes, education and training institutions, and enterprises and institutions. The system strictly adheres to national safety production laws, regulations, and industry standards, focusing on high-risk scenarios such as laboratory safety, special equipment operation, and production operations, and fully supporting the safety management mechanism of "training first, assessment later, and admission later".


The platform deeply integrates six core functions of online learning, training management, simulation exercises, online exams, score analysis, and certificate management, and constructs an integrated closed-loop management system of "teaching, learning, practice, examination, and management". Covering the entire lifecycle process of student management, course allocation, online training, learning tracking, theoretical and practical exams, electronic certificate issuance, archive filing, and review reminders, achieving digital, standardized, traceable, and quantifiable management of the entire process of safety education.


By implementing a mandatory training and assessment mechanism before admission, the system effectively enhances personnel safety literacy, standardizes operational behavior, and strengthens emergency response capabilities, laying a solid safety defense line for scientific research, teaching, and production activities, and helping units effectively fulfill their main responsibilities for safety production.
